Why Trials?

Insomia is common, but finding the right sleep solution is personal.

Our expert-guided program decodes what's keeping you awake to uncover how to get you back to sleep.

How Trials Work

1

Complete Your Online Consultation

Submit an online visit so your doctor can understand your needs and receive up 2 different trial prescriptions.

2

Pick Up Prescriptions From Your Mailbox

Try one medication for one week and the second the next week. Keep a journal of your progress along the way.

3

Follow-Up With Your Doctor

Review your journal with your doctor, who may prescribe new medications to try along with sleep hygeine advice based on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy.

4

Get Ongoing Support

Your sleep expert will continue to work with you as long as it takes to find a personalized treatment plan that fits your lifestyle and needs.
